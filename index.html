Sheridan Artwall

An art and works sharing platform for Sheridan students. Hybrid mobile application that acts very similar to Instagram, allowing students to post their artistic works and share them with other users. Includes profile creation and user authentication during login, as well as a database of posts and their likes.


Developed Using:

IntelliJ, Cordova Framework, JavaScript, Google Firebase, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS

Task Crawler

A productivity and time management android app developed in order to help users complete tasks on time; forming habits by way of a gamified reward system. Users add tasks to their to-do list and experience points and in-game items are awarded when they are completed.


Developed Using:

Android Studio, Java, Room (SQLite)

ArtWander iOS

An iOS application that allows users to create a profile and interact with the rest of Sheridan students through a dedicated social media environment, one specifically tailored to help students find others to help with their school projects or passion projects. Includes a main feed of posts, profile pages, and instant messaging among other technologies, such as geolocation, and WatchOS components.


Developed Using:

Xcode, Swift, Google Firebase
